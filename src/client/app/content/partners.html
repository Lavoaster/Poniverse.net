Partners

Poniverse has developed meaningful and lasting partnerships with several organizations through it’s time. Here you will find a brief description of each. Partnerships help to further the community goal of Poniverse and are beneficial to everyone involved!

BronyCAN, first founded in 2012, is Canada’s first pony convention! It will be held from August 19th to August 21st. BronyCAN’s mission is offer a place in which fans of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic can mingle, meet, and have an awesome time. They pride themselves on their friendliness fun activities.

Everfree Northwest, a con that first began in 2012, is the second biggest pony convention on the west coast! It is held in Seattle, where people from all over the world attend. Their sole mission is to bring Equestria to Earth for three days. And this year it will be held from May 13th to 15th.

visit now

BABSCon, the second largest pony convention in the world, is San Francisco Bay Area’s very own pony convention. It will be held from April 22nd to April 24th. BABSCon’s goal is to provide the craziest, funnest, and most awesome experience it can to fans of My Little Pony!

visit now

Nightmare Night Dallas, a family friendly brony convention, was founded in 2013. It currently runs from October 21st to 23th in Texas. Nightmare Night Dallas is dedicated to making a fantastic experiences for all types of bronies.
