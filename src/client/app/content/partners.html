Partners

Poniverse has developed meaningful and lasting partnerships with several organizations through it’s time. Here you will find a brief description of each. Partnerships help to further the community goal of Poniverse and are beneficial to everyone involved!

BronyCAN, first founded in 2012, is Canada’s first pony convention! It is held from August 21st to August 23rd. BronyCAN’s mission is offer a place in which fans of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic can mingle, meet, and have an awesome time. They pride themselves on their friendliness fun activities.

visit now

Everfree Northwest, a con that first began in 2012, is the second biggest pony convention on the west coast! It is held in Seattle, where people from all over the world attend. Their sole mission is to bring Equestria to Earth for three days.

visit now

BABSCon, the second largest pony convention in the world, is San Francisco Bay Area’s very own pony convention. It will be held from April 22nd to April 24th. BABSCon’s goal is to provide the craziest, funnest, and most awesome experience it can to fans of My Little Pony!

visit now

Nightmare Night Dallas, a family friendly brony convention, was founded in 2013. It currently runs from October 23rd to 25th in Texas. Nightmare Night Dallas is dedicated to making a fantastic experiences for all types of bronies.

visit now

Ponyville Live! is Poniverse’s first non-convention partner. Ponyville Live! is the pony world’s leading multimedia network. They host radio shows, video streams, podcasts, and other show related projects. If you want to kick back and relax while watching or listening to something pony-related, Ponyville Live! has got you covered.

visit now

Writers in Harmony is a production network dedicated to the My Little Pony community. They work to bring the world of pastel colored ponies to life through stories, music, artwork, and more! They’ve brought you gems like Dinky’s Destiny. Watch them closely as they work to bring their current masterpiece to life, Equestria Tales.
