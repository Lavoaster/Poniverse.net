Poniverse is the supercommunity that runs multiple brony fan sites. We offer multiple things a stand-alone site cannot: one universal login for every Poniverse site, global notifications from every site collected in a central dashboard, cross-site integration wherever it makes sense, and dedicated servers that stay online under heavy traffic.
