Terms of Service

Section 1 - Community Code of Conduct

The following will not be tolerated:

  • illegal or unauthorised activity
  • harassing and/or attacking Poniverse members, including Poniverse staff, via verbal, physical, written, or other abuse
  • spamming of any Poniverse services

The following is is not allowed anywhere on the Poniverse network except where explicitly permitted and directed (such as marking an uploaded Pony.fm track as “explicit”)

  • overtly sexual content
  • overtly violent or grotesque content

The following is allowed in tasteful moderation:

  • implied sexual content
  • obscene language

You must be 13 years of age or older to hold a Poniverse account.

Creating/holding multiple Poniverse accounts is forbidden. Doing so to get a new name is not a valid excuse - you can change your public display name on Poniverse's services.

A Poniverse account may only be used by the individual who created it.

Users are responsible for ALL activities that occur under their accounts.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their account and password.

All content is subject to moderation by Poniverse staff.

Section 2 - Punitive Actions

Punitive actions may differ among Poniverse services. Please check with the individual services for punitive policies specific to them.

Bans from one service do not automatically propagate to Poniverse’s other services. However, member history from one Poniverse service may be used to make a moderative decision on another.

Poniverse reserves the right to remove any user-posted content from its services at any time, for any reason, with or without notice to its author.

Poniverse reserves the right to suspend a member's access to any or all of Poniverse’s services at any time, for any reason, and for any length of time, with or without notice to the member.

Section 3 - Moderation Disputes

Because we recognize that no one is perfect, including our own staff, we provide channels for members to dispute moderative actions taken against them.

Moderation disputes are handled on a best-effort basis. Opening a dispute provides no guarantee whatsoever that actions taken against you will be reversed. Poniverse staff always have the final say.

All disputes are handled privately.

Failure to maintain the confidentiality of your dispute may invalidate your dispute and lead to further punitive action, as deemed appropriate solely by Poniverse.

Section 4 - Terms of Service Updates

Poniverse may update the TOS at any time, at its sole discretion.

Any changes to the TOS will become effective 7 days after their implementation.

Your continued use of the Poniverse websites and services constitutes an agreement to the current terms of service.
